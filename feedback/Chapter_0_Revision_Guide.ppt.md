### **Example 8: INPACT Dimensions**

**BEFORE:**
```markdown
**I - Instant:** Sub-second response times. Agents must respond at conversation speed, not batch-processing speed. Echo's 9-13 second responses killed adoption—patients hung up. The requirement isn't "fast enough"—it's "instant."

**N - Natural:** Understanding user intent in natural language. When Echo's agents understood only 40-60% of queries, users gave up after multiple rephrasings. Natural language understanding requires semantic layers that map business terminology to technical schemas.

**P - Permitted:** Dynamic, context-aware authorization. Role-based access alone is insufficient for agent scenarios. Echo's HIPAA violations occurred because their system couldn't enforce "Nurse A can access Patient X's data during her shift, but not at 3 AM from home." Agents need attribute-based access control (ABAC) layered on RBAC to evaluate context in real-time.
```

**AFTER:**
```markdown
**I - Instant:** Agents must respond at conversation speed. Echo's scheduling agent took nine to thirteen seconds—long enough for patients to hang up and call the human desk instead. "Fast enough" isn't the standard. Instant is.

**N - Natural:** Users don't speak SQL. Echo's agents understood only half of what care coordinators asked them, forcing staff to rephrase simple questions three or four times before giving up. Natural language understanding requires semantic layers that translate business terminology into technical schemas automatically.

**P - Permitted:** Role-based access can't handle context. Echo's system granted access based on job title alone—nurse, physician, administrator. It couldn't answer: Is this nurse on duty right now? Is she assigned to this patient? Is this the minimum necessary data for her task? That's why they violated HIPAA. Agents need attribute-based access control layered on traditional roles, evaluating permissions dynamically based on who's asking, when, why, and what for.
```

**CHANGES:** Rounded "40-60%" to "half", spelled out "9-13", made RBAC/ABAC explanation concrete, varied rhythm

**YOUR DECISION:** ☐ Adopt  ☐ Modify  ☐ Reject
